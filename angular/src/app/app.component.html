Hectoc Challenger

Solve hectoc challenges. Hectocs are mathematical challenges developed by Yusnier Viera. More infos at https://wir-rechnen.de/hectoc

The goal is to combine the 6 numbers to a total of 100. You can use the mathematical operations + - * / ^ and the parenthesis () Numbers can be combined but you have to use all 6 of them. This site is a private project from seism0saurus.de
