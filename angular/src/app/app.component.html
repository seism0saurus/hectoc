Hectoc Challenger

Solve hectoc challenges. Hectocs are mathematical challenges developed by Yusnier Viera. More infos at https://wir-rechnen.de/hectoc

The goal is to combine the 6 numbers to a total of 100. You can use the mathematical operations + - * / ^ and the parenthesis () Numbers can be combined but you have to use all 6 of them.

For example: 991347 can be solved with "99+1-(3+4-7)".

This site is a private project from seism0saurus.de. The algorithm to check your solution is experimental, so please help me to improve it. Please open [issues](https://github.com/seism0saurus/hectoc/issues) on the [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/seism0saurus/hectoc) or send errors to hectoc@seism0saurus.de if you don't have an account.
