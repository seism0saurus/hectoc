Hectoc

What is it?

Hectocs are mathematical challenges developed by Yusnier Viera.
More infos are available in German at https://wir-rechnen.de/hectoc
Hectocs are so popular, that a German Championship exists. There's also an open competition during the preparation days of the Junior Mental Calculation World Championship Those persons and organisations are unrelated to this project.

Rules

  1. A hectoc consists of 6 random numbers from 1-9.
  2. The goal is to combine these 6 numbers to a total of 100.
  3. You can use the mathematical operations + - * / ^ and the two parenthesis ( ).
  4. Numbers can be combined to form bigger numbers with multiple digits.
  5. You have to use all 6 of them.
  6. You must not change the order of the numbers.

Example

The hectoc is 991347.
It can be solved with 99+1-(3+4-7). The first two numbers are combined to 99. Then, 1 is added, and the result from 3+4-7 is subtracted.
But it can also be solved with 9+91+3+4-7.
